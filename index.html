About

ICT Department in SASTRA

In 2006 , B.Tech program in Information & Communication Technology was Introduced in SASTRA. This B.Tech. programme is a proper blend of communication engineering and Information Technology.

ICT@S

ICT@S (Information and Communication Technology @ SASTRA) was established in the year 2010. The association is headed by Muthaiah R and is run by the elected board members.Throughout the year, ICT@S conducts a plethora of events which engages the smartest minds across the university. ICTAS is a platform to develop skills and also to help the juniors in different aspects as well.

Tech Carnival

Tech Carnival has a plethora of competitions from a variety of streams- technical, Communication, management and fun events thereby attracting students from different fields.
